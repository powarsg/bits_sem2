determine the environment configuration. The student will: 
ΓÇó Implement a custom Drone Rescue environment.  
ΓÇó Use dynamic programming to compute the optimal value function and policy.  
ΓÇó Analyse how state design and reward design affect convergence and rescue efficiency.
